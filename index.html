Wutnow is alpha Web Usability testing software.

Here's how it works:

  • You enter our usability testing tool
  • You then view blurry screenshots of 5 web sites
  • We track your mouse movements over those images
  • This tells us which areas you found interesting enough to view
  • Once you've seen 5 shots, you upload your screenshot and it enters the testing queue
  • After your site has been seen 5 times, you get an email with your heatmap!

Can you feel the heatmaps?

The heatmap data is useful to web and interface designers, helping them to know best how to present their data by showing where users eyes go and where they don't!

With luck, we can make the web a happier and better looking place for everyone!
